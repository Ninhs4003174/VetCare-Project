Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, navigate to the Appointments page, select your preferred date and time, and confirm the booking.

Can I access my pet’s medical history online?

Yes, you can access your pet’s complete medical history, including vaccination records, treatment plans, and past consultations, through the Pet Records page.

Is my personal information secure on VetCare?

VetCare uses state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to ensure that all your personal and pet-related information is safe and secure.

Can I get reminders for my pet's medication schedules?

Yes, VetCare allows you to set up reminders for your pet's medication schedules to ensure timely administration of treatments.

How do I update my pet’s records?

You can update your pet's records by logging into your account, navigating to the Pet Records section, and editing the necessary information.
