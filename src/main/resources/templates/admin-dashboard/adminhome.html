Welcome to Your Admin Dashboard

As an administrator, you have the ability to manage users, clinics, vets, and various other resources within the system. Use the navigation above to access different sections of the platform.

Your oversight is critical in ensuring the smooth operation of services. Whether you're adding new clinics, approving resources, or managing user accounts, your attention helps keep everything running seamlessly.

Admin Tips

  • User Management: Regularly review user activity to ensure only verified users are active on the system.
  • Clinic Management: Keep clinic information accurate and up-to-date so users can easily find the services they need.
  • Vet Approvals: Periodically check vet credentials to maintain trust and credibility within the platform.
  • Resource Approvals: Quickly approve or deny resource submissions to keep the content relevant.
